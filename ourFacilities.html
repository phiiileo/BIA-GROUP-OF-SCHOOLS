OUR FACILITIES

BIA group of schools provides modern facilities in and outside the classroom, which are utilized to give maximum benefits to the pupils in all academic and recreational activities. Modern audio visual teaching aids including video/cd machine and computer based equipment are made available for better understanding of some particular subjects. All classrooms are fully equipped such that class sessions are always practical, exciting and most fulfilling.

ICT+

Change is the only thing that is lasting in the world. BIA group of schools appreciate the importance of computer education and that is why it is made compulsory for all students. There are enough system units to accomodate a full class with each pupil having unhindered access to develop his or her skill. The great technology of teaching through interactive white board is one of our greatest strength and our computer room is fully equipped with internet access.

SCHOOL CLINIC +

The school clinic is well equipped. It is manned by a qualified medical doctor with certified nurses. Minor ailments developed by learners in the school are attended to at no cost. Serious cases are referred to hospital in the neighbourhood while the cost of treatment will be settled by the learner's parents. It is important that parents furnish the school with adequate medical history of the learner. In addition newly admitted learners will unergo medical examination.

LIBRARY +

The school library has a well stocked library into which the pupils have acces. The librarian is always available to help the learners on how to put the library to good use. The library is spacious for the comfort of its users and stocked with wide range of books.

HOME ECONOMICS LAB+

The school has well developed Home Economics lab that is aimed at helping learners develop manipulating and improvising skills in the area of food and nutrition practices, needlework and craftwork.

ART ROOM+

Art is the expression of creativity, pleasure in dexterity, invention and ingenious use of natural materials. The school has a well euipped art room and qualified teachers to train the learners. The children express their creative capabilities and strive as much as they can to sharpen their skills and talent as well as stimulates imagination.
