Preparing A Generation To Make A Difference_

The passion for character formation, impacting knowledge and building excellent educational career in children led the director of the school to establish a pre-primary school (BIA Montessori School) that can foster brilliance, boldness and integrity in our community which was officially opened on 17th September 2007.

BIA College is an off-shoot of BIA Montessori School, founded in 2014 to provide excellent training and education at the crucial point of learning. In view of landmark achievements by the pupils of Montessori School, the imperative for stability and continuity in character formation and knowledge acquisition led to the emergence of BIA Schools as a post Primary Education Acaemy in September 2014. The board of Governors decided to add as secondary school to foster brilliance, boldness and integrity in addition to providing best education for the learners.

BIA School is a co-educational day school and is situated within th seren environment of Araromi, Along Badagry Expressway, Lagos State, Nigeria.

Our Mission

To provide academic and moral education that bring about succesful careers using the relevant curriculum and maintenace of high standard of education in all fields.

Our Vision

To produce quality learners that will impact knowledge to the world.
