THE CURRICULUM

In complaince with statutory requirements, the academy prepares learners through the second and third stage of the education system adopted in Nigeria. These consist of grades 7 to 12 (year 7 through 12) or Lower Basic (Check NCCR). The board based curriculum is in line with the 9-3-4 system of Education.
In the first three years of secondary school, all learners will study a full range of acadmic subject and participate in creative subjects and sporting activities. After the first 3 years, learners sit for Junior School Examination which qualifies them for the last 3 years of stay in the school. Entery into the year 10 (i.e grade 10 or SS1) is dependent on a reasonably good performance at the Junior Secondary Certifcation Examination.
As a result our learners are well equipped for admission into any higher institution both in Nigeria and Abroad.
Final Year learners of BIA Group of Schools are encourage to sit for the Senior Secondary Certification Examination (SSCE), NECO and other related external examinations.

CLUB & SOCIETY

BIA Group of Schools believes properly planned and managed clubs and societies have immense value in fostering school spirit and morale including adding to pleasure of the education ecperiences. The school therefore encourages new skill and interest in her pupils through clubs and societies.
Regular Clubs and Societies include:

  • Football
  • Literary and Debating Society
  • Boys Scout
  • Red Cross Society
  • Jet club
  • Boys/Girls Brigade
  • Computer Club
  • Press Club
  • Music and Drama Club

Membership to any of these clubs is opened to the entire learners who may be ask to pay registration fee.
Apart from club activities, parties are also organized for the children at the end of the school year and Christmas time. Some international days are also observed e.g Mother's day, Father's day, Children's day and the likes.
