Our C.E.O

Ajayi Adekunle P. (Mr.)
Chief Executive Director

Preparing A Generation To Make A Difference

With gratitude to God the creator of the heaven and earth and with so much joy and happiness in my heart, I say welcome to this lovely school, where each child is accepted and appreciated for his/her uniqueness.
I'm aware of the tremendious opportunity given to us in creating this life transforming institution. We have the opportunity to instill a love of learning that can be nurtured and empowered to grow into something truly beautiful.

My staffs show enough care to make our children feel more comfortable and learn successfully in a conducive and friendly environment. We work together with all efforts to build a warm, welcoming and nurturing environment for our children.

Pursuing a higher standard of learning and giving a quantitative and qualitative education in times like this, this is my priority for the school. About 80% of our learners are from different backgrounds and cultures. We all enjoy the benefits from this cultural diversity. Teaching moral, civic values and other societal qualities which make our students outsanding while encountering challenges in life are also important aspects of our teaching in BIA GROUP OF SCHOOLS.

Been able to create this tremendious environment that facilitates learning processes in not by might but by the grace given to us by God and it's our responsibiity to keep up so we can continue to foster standard educational training in our community. Our school is located in a serene environment of Elijah Bus Stop, Araromi Ale Phase II, Badagry Expressway, Lagos.

It's a privilege, honour and responsibility to take care of this gift.
